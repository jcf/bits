* Goal
Render the Posts tab with different post types: free posts, purchasable posts
(locked/owned states), and subscriber-only posts. Handle like actions and
display download areas for owned content.

* Reference
~.claude/references/purchase-flow-reference.html~ — Lines 1048-1268

* Post Types
| Type            | Access               | Locked State                 |
|-----------------+----------------------+------------------------------|
| Free            | Everyone             | N/A                          |
| Purchasable     | Purchase required    | Blurred preview + price      |
| Subscriber-only | Subscription required| Blurred + "Subscribe" prompt |

* Data Model
** Post access level (idents)
#+begin_src clojure

{:db/ident :post.access/purchase}

|---------------------+---------+---------------------------------|

** post-header
Avatar, display name, relative timestamp. Shows price badge for purchasable.

** post-actions
Like button uses ~bits.form/form~. Comment button (display only for now).

** post-locked
Blurred overlay with price and unlock button. Uses ~bits.form/form~.

** post-subscriber-locked
Blurred overlay with lock icon and subscribe button. Uses ~bits.form/form~.

** post-owned
Full content visible with download area showing filename and download button.

** post-card
Composes header, body, content area (based on access/ownership), and actions.

** mid-feed-cta
Dashed border card encouraging subscription. Inserted after 2-3 posts.
