About

I've been a photographer for 15 years, working across editorial fashion, fine art, and fetish photography. My work explores the tension between material and body — how surfaces interact, how light responds to texture, how stillness communicates more than movement.

Everything published here is produced with full consent and collaboration from every person involved. I pay my collaborators fairly and credit them in every set. If you've ever wondered what ethical, professional fetish art looks like — this is it.

Purchasing directly from me means no platform takes a cut of your support. Every pound goes to equipment, studio time, travel, and paying the people who make this work possible.
